About this tool

KOL Finder — Seeding Tool

This tool helps you identify strong Instagram seeding candidates by automatically scoring and ranking accounts based on how well they match your target niche and how actively engaged their audience is. It works by analysing posts from accounts that have tagged or engaged with a competitor's content — giving you a ranked shortlist of KOLs already active in your space.

You can bring in accounts two ways: upload an .xlsx export from Apify, or paste competitor post URLs and hashtags directly — the tool triggers the scrape for you and skips the manual Apify step entirely. Either way, it deduplicates accounts, scores them across Engagement and Relevancy, and produces a ranked table ready to filter, review, and export to Excel.

Optionally, run AI Deep-Dive on the top results — this sends account captions, hashtags, bio, and your campaign brief to Claude and returns a qualitative verdict per account. Base scoring is deterministic arithmetic computed entirely in your browser; AI is only involved if you explicitly opt in.

How to use

Seeding Tool — step by step

1

Bring in your account data — pick one of two paths:

Option A — paste URLs / hashtags

Use this when you're starting a fresh scrape right now.

Go to Seeder → "Scrape URLs / Hashtags". Paste competitor post URLs, brand-tagged pages, or hashtags (one per line — #skincare or just skincare). Choose a result limit and click Start scrape. The tool calls Apify, polls until done, and feeds results straight into the pipeline.

Option B — upload XLSX

Use this when you already have a previous Apify export.

If you ran an Apify Instagram Scraper job previously and saved the .xlsx, go to Seeder → "Upload XLSX" and drop the file in. The filename becomes the brand label in the export. Upload multiple files to combine brands. Also useful to re-score an old dataset with different niche filters.

Important — costs

Fetch Live Stats triggers a live Apify scrape — roughly $0.01 per account. A typical run of 300–400 accounts costs ~$3–4. Results are cached 7 days, so re-uploading the same dataset within that window won't re-scrape.

Direct scrape (Option A intake) also uses Apify — cost scales with result limit. 200 results ≈ $0.50–1; 1,000 results ≈ $2–3.

AI Deep-Dive calls Claude — approximately $0.01–0.05 per run depending on account count and caption length. Verdicts are cached 7 days per account + brief, so re-running the same brief won't re-charge processed accounts.

Scoring methodology

How accounts are scored

Formula
Overall (0–100)= Engagement Score × 8 + Relevancy Score × 2
Engagement= log(1 + medianLikes + medianViews × 0.5) · after live fetch
Engagement= log(1 + avgLikes + avgComments × 3) · before live fetch
Relevancy= 5 + keyword hits − off-niche category hits · capped 0–10
